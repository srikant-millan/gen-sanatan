Short term courses for working professionals (2 - 16 hours)


Data Science for Professionals

An overview of Data Science methods and techniques (2 hours)

This is the age of Information and Data. We believe that every professional needs to be a data literate. This is a two hours program designed for individual contributors, junior to mid-level professionals coming from either private or public sectors seeking to manage or will manage data science teams or vendors.

You will get to know the overview of data processing techniques, some mathematical and statistical terms and tools used in a data science project and the principles and practices of data science in industry. We will demonstrate few hands-on example how inferences are drawn from the data to make decisions.

Data Science for Managers

An overview of how data-based decisions can drive your business (2 hours)

This is a non-technical and non-mathematical overview of data science designed for project managers, product managers, R&D managers and CEOs. This is a detailed overview mainly to explain how you can make better business decions for your company using the inferences drawn from the historical data.

We will demonstrate different tools and techniques used for data analysis. You will see several examples and stories explainign dat driven business decisions. Also, get familiar with beautiful and meaningful graphs and plots for your business reporting hod how effective they are.
